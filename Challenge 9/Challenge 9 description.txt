Challenge 9 Number Analyzer:
Read one integer from the user and print a full analysis.

Detect and print:
Is it positive, negative, or zero?
Is it even or odd?
Is it a prime number?
Is it a perfect square?
Sum of its digits (use absolute value)
The number reversed  (e.g. 1234 → 4321)

Example — input: 169

Property	      		Result
Positive?	       		Yes
Even/Odd?		Odd
Prime?	        	No
Perfect Square?	 	Yes  (13 × 13)
Digit Sum			16
Reversed			961
